Competition Rules

Challenges

Players can only issue one challenge at a time.
Players can only challenge players on the level directly above them.
Players can only be involved in one active challenge per competition.

Rechallenges

After losing (or forfeiting) a match, the loser must wait a period of time before they can challenge the winner again.

Number of days before rechallenge:

{{vm.competition.waitingPeriodDays}} days

Forfeits

If a challenge result has not been entered after a period of time, the opponent forfeits and swicthes places with the challenger in the competition.

Number of days before forfeit:

{{vm.competition.forfeitDays}} days

Holds

Players can place themselves on hold when they are unable to play matches (ex. out of the office).
Players on hold can not be challenges and cannot challenge other players.
Players must remove the hold before they can resume participation in the competition.
Holds should not be used to avoid challenges from particular players.

Weekends

Challenges are not allowed on weekends.
All incomplete (result not entered) challenges will be cancelled on Friday at 5:00pm.
Players can resume issuing challenges on Monday.

Disputes

If you have a dispute regarding a challenge or rules violations you can address it with a competition admin.
